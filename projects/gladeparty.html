Glade Party

This game is not yet finished, this is a Work In Progress.

Project Type: University Project
Software Used: Unity3D
Languages Used: C#
Roles: Lead Programmer

About

Glade Party is a Couch-Multiplayer Party Game where players throw their dices in order to make their way through the board of the enchanted forest in dangered by the polution in the world. In the end of each round players compete in one of many minigames, in order to get coins to invest in the population on different areas of the board. The winner is the one who contributed the most to the development of the enchanted forest.

Roles

Lead Programmer

As the Lead Programmer, I was responsible for managing the Programming Team. The team end up consisting of myself and Lucas (check his art station). Together we made all the shaders, architecture, gameplay, offline multiplayer and UI backend of the game.
