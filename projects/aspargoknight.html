Aspargo Knight

This game is not yet finished, this is a Work In Progress.

Project Type: University Project
Software Used: Unity3D
Languages Used: C#
Roles: Programmer

About

Aspargo Knight is a 2D Side-Scroller Platformer game about a banana knight that lost his memory and is trying to understand why the vegetables are in war the fruits. Fight your way through the food kingdom by defeating the mean vegetables that try to stop you on your epic journey!

Roles

Programmer

As the only programmer in the team, I was responsible to implement all game mechanics including the player controller with the jumping and walking mechanic as well as the enemy AI and their respective animations.
