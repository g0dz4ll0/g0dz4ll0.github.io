Call of Pets - Modern Wizard

Project Type: Personal Project
Software Used: Unity3D
Languages Used: C#
Roles: Programmer

About

Call of Pets - Modern Wizard is a Comic 3D Top-Down 3rd Person Shooter about a wizard that tries to save his little elemental friends from mean monsters that are invading his realm. Fight your way through the different realms against waves of mean monsters that emprisoned your elemental friends, save them and get their help through your journey by using their hidden powers. The game was made for the 2022.2 Barckeys GameJam in 1 day which the theme was "You're Not Alone".

Roles

Gameplay Programmer

As the Gameplay Programmer, I was responsible to work and implement most of the game mechanics like shooting, player movement, dialogue system and wave system. I also developed the enemy AI and helped develop some of the buffs in the game.
