Project Type: University Project
Software Used: Unity3D

About

Ctrl/Esc is a Survival Horror Game. You're a technician, assigned to fix stuff in a school during summer. While exploring all the rooms, you might discover some weird information and strange things may happen.

Roles

Lead Programmer

As the Lead Programmer, I was responsible for managing the Programming Team. The team consisted of me and Lucas (check his art station). We used Peer Programming and Extreme Programming in order to get the project done in under 6 weeks.
