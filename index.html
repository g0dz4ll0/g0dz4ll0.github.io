About Me

Hello, my name is Gonçalo Oliveira, also known as God. Born in 1999, Graduated in Game Design at IPB in 2022. In this website I'm showing you all the stuff I've been doing during my Game Dev Journey.

My main interests are Game Engines, Tools and Procedural Content Generation. I love creating the platforms that help my colleagues produce the wonderful worlds, stories and experiences that the players should be allowed to explore and feel.
